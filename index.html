About Me

I'm a multifaceted artist who specializes in graphic design. I spend my time dreaming and creating a more beautiful world for us to look at through a digital medium. On the evenings you can find me cycling through the beautiful Wenatchee Valley that I'm lucky to call home. Other than that I'm probably learning or perfecting a skill. I'm a life-long learner and I continually advance in my experiences and skills throughout life.
